About Me

Hi, my name is Anthony Keo. I am a Seattle based visual designer currently studying at The Art Institute of Seattle. I enjoy creating simple yet engaging imagery that allows me to have fun with color, shape, layout and typography. I've always agreed with the idea that content should define the appearance and to never be too married with a specific style or approach. This site provides me with the outlet to grow, experiment and absorb my profession. I hope I can give back as much as I take and inspire people to persevere with their own passion.
